Milkha Singh

" The Flying Sikh "

The Timeline of Milkha Singh

  • 1929: Born on Novemeber 20 in Govindpura, Punjab, British India.

  • 1951: Joins the Indian Army and begins his athletic career as a sprinter.

  • 1958: Wins gold medals in the athletics events of the 1958 and 1962 Asian Games.

  • 1958: Awarded the Padma Shri, India's fourth-highest civilian honour.

  • 1960: Participates in the 1960 Summer Olympics in Rome, narrowly missing a medal in the 400m race, finishing fourth.

  • 1969: Retires from athletics and later serves in various administrative roles related to sports.

  • He passed away on June 18, 2021 due to complications from COVID-19.

" You can achieve anything in life. It just depends on how desperate you are to achieve it."-- Milkha Singh
